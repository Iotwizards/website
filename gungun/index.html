Who we are?

The purpose of IOT WIZARDS club is to promote and organize project skills and experience at MIET. The club aims to bring in collabration form the students so as to build products or provide services that solve a common problem or give shape to an innovative idea while working in teams and learning with experience. We are IOT WIZARDS club. The programatic and progressive computer science club of MIET. We bring a more sophisticated approach to student centered college bodies along with transparency in governance.
